California, located in the western region of the United States, stands as the most populous state in the nation. With a diverse and vibrant population, California is renowned for its cultural and geographic diversity, encompassing everything from stunning coastal landscapes to sprawling urban centers. The state's history dates back to its admission into the Union on September 9, 1850, becoming the 31st state of the United States. California's economy is one of the largest in the world, driven by industries like technology, entertainment, agriculture, and tourism. Its average income level is relatively higher than the national average, with a median household income around $80,000, though this can vary significantly between different regions within the state. This dynamic state continues to attract people from all walks of life, drawn by its opportunities, innovation, and unique way of life.
